Yash

Naveen Kumar Gowda (born 8 January 1986), known by his stage name Yash,is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Kannada cinema. He is a recipient of three Filmfare Awards South and two SIIMA awards. Yash began his career with appearances in several television series in the 2000s. He made his film debut in 2007 with Jambada Hudugi. The 2008 romantic drama Moggina Manasu, for which he received the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actor, proved to be a breakthrough role for Yash.
